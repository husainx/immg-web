Work Visa
Service

A Work Visa is a permit issued for a foreigner to stay in Maldives for the purpose of employment. Employers must obtain a Work Visa for their employees within 15 days of their arrival in the Maldives, based on a valid Work Permit issued by the Ministry of Homeland Security and Technology. Work Visa holders are required to maintain a valid visa at all times while residing and working in the Maldives. Work Visa holders are not permitted to depart the country without a valid visa stamped in their passport.

Work Visa Service Information

Work Visa Information

Documents Required

  • Passport-size photo (as per Immigration photo standards)
  • Passport copy (biodata page)

How to Apply

  • Log into the Xpat Online System: https://workpermit.egov.mv
  • Search for the employee using the Work Permit number or passport number
  • Go to the Visa tab and select 'New Work Visa'
  • Confirm the visa duration (same as the duration of the Work Permit)
  • Attach all required documents
  • Save and submit the application

Note: An employee must obtain a Work Visa within 15 days of arrival in the Maldives on a valid Work Permit issued by the Ministry of Homeland Security and Technology.

If the Application is Incomplete

  • The employer can view the reason for incompletion by logging into the Xpat system
  • Resubmit the incomplete documents and update the application

How to Get an Appointment for Passport Submission

  • 1.
    When the visa is approved, go to the 'Work Visa' tab and select 'Ready for Submission'
  • 2.
    Select the passport and click 'Request Appointment'
  • 3.
    Choose the submission location, preferred date, and available time slot
  • 4.
    Click 'Make Booking', then 'Confirm Booking'
  • 5.
    On the appointment date, take a token number via the 'Ant Queue' application between 8:15 AM – 10:30 AM to submit the passport

Passport Collection

  • Once the application is processed, it will appear as 'Ready for Collection' in the Xpat system
  • Tokens for collection must be taken from the 'Ant Queue' app between 8:15 AM – 10:30 AM
  • Service for collecting passports begins at 8:15 AM for five or fewer passports, and at 1:00 PM for six or more passports

Important Notes

  • No service will be provided for missed tokens, unless urgency is verified via email
  • Do not take multiple tokens for the same submission receipt or PPH number
  • Information tokens will be issued from 8:15 AM to 11:45 AM, and from 1:00 PM to 1:45 PM
  • If you miss your passport submission appointment, please reschedule it through the Xpat Online System
