Dependent Visa
Application

A Dependent Visa is issued to immediate family members of expatriates in professional categories working in the Maldives. Before arrival, apply for a Sponsorship Declaration via the IMUGA portal at least 5 working days before arrival. After arrival on sponsorship, apply for a Dependent Visa.

Dependent Visa Requirements and Process

Quick Navigation

Documents Required

  • Application Form (IM23)
  • Passport-size photo (per Immigration photo standards)
  • Passport copy (biodata page)
  • Last arrival stamp (for new applicants)
  • Last visa page (for renewals)
  • Relationship proof document - Documents proving the relationship with the sponsored person
  • Medical report
  • Health insurance
  • Visa fee: MVR 750, Card fee: MVR 50
  • Additional documents (if applicable)

Sponsor Requirements

  • Valid residence status in the Maldives (citizen, permanent resident, or work visa holder)
  • Sufficient financial means to support dependents (minimum salary requirement applies)
  • Adequate accommodation for family members
  • No criminal record or immigration violations

Required Documents

  • Dependent's valid passport with minimum 6 months validity
  • Sponsor's identification documents and residence permit
  • Relationship proof (birth certificate, marriage certificate, or adoption papers)
  • Financial support documentation (salary certificates, bank statements)
  • Medical certificate and health screening results
  • Police clearance certificate from country of residence
  • Passport-sized photographs (per Immigration photo standards)

Application Process (step-by-step)

  • 1.
    Visit imuga.immigration.gov.mv
  • 2.
    Select 'Visa'
  • 3.
    Sign in to the IMUGA portal
  • 4.
    Click 'Apply for a Visa'
  • 5.
    Confirm whether you are currently in the Maldives by completing the prerequisite field
  • 6.
    Select visa type: 'Dependent Visa' and select reason or subtype
  • 7.
    Fill in the application details
  • 8.
    Attach required documents
  • 9.
    Review and submit the application

Visa Renewal & Extension

  • Dependent visa validity is typically linked to sponsor's visa duration
  • Renewal must be applied for before current visa expires
  • Continued proof of relationship and financial support required
  • Children may need to apply for independent status upon reaching 18

Important Notes

  • If appointment is missed, reschedule the appointment via email permits@immigration.gov.mv or contact hotline 9199157
  • An appointment time will be provided via SMS to submit the passport for visa issuance
  • When applying for Sponsorship Declaration, the stated purpose must be to bring in a dependent in order to qualify for a Dependent Visa
