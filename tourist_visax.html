Work Visa

Employers must secure and maintain valid Work Visas for foreign employees within 15 days of arrival via the Xpat system.

Passport & Travel Documents

  • A valid passport or travel document with a Machine Readable Zone (MRZ)
  • Minimum remaining validity of one month beyond the date of arrival
    Note: Passports showing an "extended validity" endorsement are not accepted
  • Exception: As of 15 April 2025, entry on Israeli passports is prohibited (Immigration Act 1/2007, 3rd Amendment)

Travel Itinerary & Accommodation

  • Confirmed return or onward tickets
  • Pre-paid booking at a Maldives-registered hotel

Traveller Declaration

Health Declaration

  • Yellow Fever Vaccination Certificate, if your journey originates from a Yellow Fever endemic country
  • Children under one year are exempt
  • Check the current list of Yellow Fever countries before departure
