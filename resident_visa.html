Resident Visa
Application

A Resident Visa is granted to a foreign national married to a Maldivian citizen, allowing them to reside in the Maldives. Foreigners coming to marry a Maldivian or to register their marriage must be sponsored before arrival. Submit a Sponsorship Declaration via the IMUGA portal.

Resident Visa Requirements and Process

Quick Navigation

After Arrival

  • Complete the legal marriage registration process
  • Apply for a Marriage Visa within the given timeframe
  • If already in the Maldives on another visa, apply for a visa transfer after registration

How to Apply

  • Visit imuga.immigration.gov.mv
  • Select 'Visa'
  • Sign in to the IMUGA portal
  • Click 'Apply for a Visa'
  • Confirm whether you are currently in the Maldives by completing the prerequisite field
  • Select visa type: 'Resident Visa' and select reason or subtype
  • Fill in the next application details
  • Attach required documents
  • Review and submit the application

Required Documents

  • Valid passport with minimum 12 months validity
  • Proof of continuous residence in the Maldives (visa history, employment records)
  • Financial documentation (investment proof, bank statements, tax records)
  • Police clearance certificate from country of origin and Maldives
  • Medical certificate and health insurance coverage
  • Language proficiency certificate (Dhivehi or English)
  • Character references and community integration proof

Application Process (step-by-step)

  • 1.
    Determine eligibility category and gather preliminary documents
  • 2.
    Consult with Immigration Department for pre-application assessment
  • 3.
    Complete comprehensive application form with all supporting documents
  • 4.
    Submit application with full documentation and pay processing fees
  • 5.
    Undergo background checks, interviews, and assessment by relevant committees
  • 6.
    Receive decision from Immigration Department
    Note: Processing time is typically 6-12 months depending on category.

Rights and Obligations

  • Right to live and work permanently in the Maldives
  • Access to public services and social benefits (limited)
  • Obligation to comply with all Maldivian laws and regulations
  • Requirement to maintain investment/residence conditions
  • Option to apply for citizenship after additional qualifying period

Important Notes

  • After approval, you will receive an SMS notification with an appointment time to submit the passport
  • Visa card fee: MVR 50
  • Initial Marriage Visa validity: 1 year, Renewals: Up to 5 years
  • Apply for extensions within 3 months before expiry via the IMUGA portal
