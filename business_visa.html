Business Visa
Application

Business visa applications must be submitted to the Ministry of Economic Development through the Business Portal: http://business.egov.mv. Upon submission, a General Service Request (GSR) number will be generated, and Maldives Immigration will process the application after Ministry approval.

Business Visa Requirements and Process

Quick Navigation

Verification Process

  • Passport Copy: A clear, color copy of the foreign applicant's passport data page, with a minimum validity of six (6) months
  • Recent Photograph: A passport-sized photo taken within the last three (3) months, meeting the photo standards accepted by Maldives Immigration
  • Visa Status Check: The applicant must not hold any other visa type specified under Article 9 of Law No. 1/2007 (Maldives Immigration Act)
  • Entry Prohibition Check: The applicant must not fall under any category of individuals prohibited from entering the Maldives as specified in Article 8 of Law No. 1/2007
  • Visa Duration Limits: Business Visas may be granted for a period of 30 to 90 days at a time, not exceeding a total of 180 days per calendar year

Required Documents

  • Valid passport with minimum 6 months validity
  • Business invitation letter from Maldivian company or organization
  • Company registration and business profile documents
  • Travel itinerary and purpose of visit documentation
  • Proof of accommodation arrangements
  • Return or onward ticket confirmation
  • Financial proof (bank statements, travel insurance)
  • Passport-sized photographs (per Immigration photo standards)

Application Process (step-by-step)

  • 1.
    Obtain invitation letter from Maldivian business partner or organization
  • 2.
    Complete business visa application form
  • 3.
    Gather all required documents and supporting materials
  • 4.
    Submit application to nearest Maldivian embassy or consulate
  • 5.
    Pay visa application and processing fees
  • 6.
    Attend interview if required by consular officers
  • 7.
    Receive visa decision and collect passport if approved
    Note: Processing time is typically 10-15 working days.

Visa Validity & Duration

  • Business visa is typically valid for 30 days from entry
  • Single or multiple entry options available based on purpose
  • Extension possible in exceptional circumstances with proper justification
  • Visa validity period starts from date of issue, not entry

Important Notes

  • Business visa does not permit employment or paid work
  • Visitor must leave the Maldives upon visa expiry
  • All business activities must comply with Maldivian laws and regulations
  • Invitation letter must be from a registered business in the Maldives
